Greek Salad

Description

  • 4 cups chopped tomatoes (about 4 medium tomatoes)
  • 1 cucumber, sliced into half-moons
  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, sliced
  • 1/2 cup Kalamata olives, pitted
  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 tbsp red wine vinegar
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  1. Chop the tomatoes into bite-sized pieces. Slice the cucumber into half-moons. Thinly slice the red onion. Slice the green bell pepper into rings or strips.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, green bell pepper, and Kalamata olives.
  3. Crumble the feta cheese over the salad.
  4. In a small bowl, whisk together the extra-virgin olive oil, red wine vinegar, dried oregano, salt, and black pepper.
  5. Pour the dressing over the salad and gently toss to combine.
  6. Serve the Greek salad immediately, or let it sit for a few minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.
